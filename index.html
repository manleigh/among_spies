Welcome to

A game where you test your musical memory in rapid fire rounds!

Instructions

  1. Log in or register!
  2. Choose a genre of music to play with. Each game has 10 randomly selected songs from that category!
  3. A brief song clip will play, you'll have to either guess the artist or the song title as fast as you can. Just fill in the textbox with your answer and click guess: the quicker you finish, the higher your score!
