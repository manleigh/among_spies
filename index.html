Welcome to Guessify

Guessify: a game where you identify the artist or song title while listening to a 30 second clip

Instructions

After logging in (or signing up if you haven't already), you'll be directed to a ___ page where you can ___!

One round is 10 songs!

  1. Choose a genre of music
  2. A 30 second song clip will play, during this time you'll either guess the artist or the song title (just fill in the textbox with your guess and click guess!)
